Management Domain: Operations

1) Consider a situation where your entire folder of permission documents has been last. All permissions ranging from venue permission, to sponsorship bonds, to overnight permissions were included in that folder. What do you do one day before the event?
2) You're conducting an event but lots of people start standing up and leaving. What do you do?
3) Consider that speakers from two reknowned companies, A & B, have agreed to send speakers for your event but you only need one. Your group has already worked with company A before and are on good terms while this will be your first time collaborating with company B. Company B is more well known than A. Which company's speaker would you choose and what would you tell to the other company that you didn't choose?
4) You go to get a room permission but the HOD refuses to give you the keys and tells you, "Do whatever you want to do, break the lock but I'm not giving you my rooms keys!" What do you do?
5) You're the coordinator of an event with incredibly low registration count days before the event. Would you scrap the event or continue with it with changes in the way you went about it originally? If you decided to go with it, what would you do differently?
6) Tell us one of your qualities which sets you apart and should be a reason to advance you to Round 2?
7) Tell us a reason we shouldn't advance you to Round 2?
8) What are the three things you look for in a chapter before you try out for it?
9) Sell me a torn Rs. 100 note.
10) You are selected into your top 2 choices of chapters in your desired domain. Both the chapters have events on the same day and you are overloaded with work. What do you do?
